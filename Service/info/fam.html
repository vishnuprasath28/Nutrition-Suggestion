FAMILY WELLNESS BUNDLE

ABOUT THIS PACKAGE

The "Family Wellness Bundle" is a comprehensive package designed to provide nutritional guidance for all family members, promoting a balanced and healthy diet for everyone. The typical duration of this program is 60 days, allowing ample time for each family member to benefit from the guidance and support. As for the pricing of the "Family Wellness Bundle," it will depend on several factors, including the number of family members included in the program, the level of personalization for each member, and any additional services offered. For accurate and up-to-date pricing information, it's best to contact us directly. Our team will be delighted to provide you with detailed information about the package and its cost, tailored to the specific needs and size of your family.
